The Doll

You are walking home from work one October evening, down a barren street. The wind carries brown leaves across the road. As you approach the next street light you notice a doll in the middle of the sidewalk under the spotlight. It seems to pull you towards it but you hesitate. You feel your hair stand up because of its presence.

Do You:

  1. Approach and pickup the doll out of curiosity?
  2. Walk to the other side of the street to avoid the creepy doll?
  3. Stay put, frozen in fear?
