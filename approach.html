Picked Up Doll

You continue to walk forward towards the doll. It just sits there, still but you swear that it’s eyes follow your every move. ‘I should really drink more water,’ you think. You stop a couple of steps in front of the doll. It still remains motionless but it’s even creepier up close. You bend down and pick up the doll. Nothing happens — not that anything should happen. But before you know it you feel your vision blurring.

Do You:

  1. Drop the doll?
  2. Keep hold of the doll as you black out?
